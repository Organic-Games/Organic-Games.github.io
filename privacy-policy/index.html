Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in Organic Games software.

We Do Not Collect Personal Information in Our Software

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in our software. Login information for third-party services is not personal and is only saved for ease of use. We only use third-party services for achievements, scoreboards, et cetera. We do not use third-party advertising services.

Our Software Complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act

Our software does not collect personal information from anybody, including children under the age of 13.

Contact Us

Contact us at info@organic.games if you have questions about this Privacy Policy.
